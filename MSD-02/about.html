About Us

Our Mission

The Government Birth Registration Portal is a comprehensive digital platform designed to streamline and modernize the birth registration process across India. Our mission is to provide accessible, secure, and efficient birth registration services to all citizens.

What We Do

We facilitate the registration of births, issuance of birth certificates, and maintenance of vital records in accordance with the Registration of Births and Deaths Act, 1969. Our portal serves as a single point of access for all birth registration related services.

Key Features

  • Online birth registration
  • Digital document upload and verification
  • Real-time application tracking
  • Secure certificate generation
  • 24/7 accessibility
  • Multi-language support

Our Commitment

We are committed to maintaining the highest standards of data security, privacy, and service quality. Our platform adheres to all government regulations and security protocols to ensure the safety of your personal information.
