Frequently Asked Questions

How long does birth registration take?

Birth registration typically takes 3-5 working days for processing and verification. You will receive a notification once your application is approved.

What documents are required for birth registration?

Required documents include hospital discharge certificate, parent's ID proof, address proof, and marriage certificate (if applicable). See our Requisite Documents page for complete details.

Can I register a birth if I missed the deadline?

Yes, you can register births up to 21 years after the event. However, additional documentation and verification may be required for delayed registrations.

How do I track my application?

You can track your application using the application number provided during registration. Log into your account and visit the dashboard to check the current status.
