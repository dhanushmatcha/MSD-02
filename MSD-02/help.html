Frequently Asked Questions

What documents are required for birth registration? ▼
You will need: Hospital discharge summary, Parent's Aadhaar cards, Marriage certificate (if applicable), Address proof, and any other documents as specified by your state government.
How long does the registration process take? ▼
Typically, the registration process takes 7-15 working days for approval. You will receive notifications at each stage of the process.
Can I register a birth if the child is older than 1 year? ▼
Yes, but additional documentation and fees may apply. Please contact your local registrar office for specific requirements in your area.
What if I find an error in my birth certificate? ▼
You can apply for corrections through the portal. Submit the required documents and pay the correction fee. The process usually takes 10-15 working days.
Is birth registration mandatory? ▼
Yes, birth registration is mandatory under the Registration of Births and Deaths Act, 1969. It is essential for obtaining various government services and documents.

Registration Process

  1. Fill out the birth registration form with accurate information
  2. Upload required documents (Aadhaar, hospital records, etc.)
  3. Submit the form for verification
  4. Wait for approval from the registrar office
  5. Download your birth certificate once approved

Required Documents

Hospital Records

Discharge summary, birth report

Parent's Aadhaar

Both parents' Aadhaar cards

Address Proof

Utility bills, rental agreement

Marriage Certificate

If parents are married
