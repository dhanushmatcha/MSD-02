Requisite Documents for Birth Registration

Essential Documents (Mandatory)

1. Hospital Discharge Certificate

Purpose: Proof of birth from medical institution

Format: Original certificate from hospital/clinic

Note: Must be issued within 21 days of birth

2. Aadhaar Card

Purpose: Identity verification of parents

Format: Original Aadhaar card or e-Aadhaar

Note: Both parents' Aadhaar cards required

3. Address Proof

Purpose: Verification of residential address

Accepted Documents:

  • Utility bills (electricity, water, gas)
  • Rental agreement
  • Property documents
  • Bank statements
  • Voter ID card

4. Phone Number

Purpose: Communication and verification

Format: Active mobile number

Note: OTP will be sent for verification

Additional Documents (If Applicable)

5. Income Certificate

Purpose: Fee calculation and subsidy eligibility

Format: Income certificate from competent authority

Note: Required for fee waiver applications

6. Marriage Certificate

Purpose: Proof of parents' marital status

Format: Original marriage certificate

Note: Required if parents are married

7. Parent's ID Proof

Purpose: Parent identification

Accepted Documents:

  • PAN Card
  • Driving License
  • Passport
  • Voter ID
  • Government ID cards

8. Birth Affidavit (if applicable)

Purpose: For delayed registrations

Format: Notarized affidavit

Note: Required for registrations after 21 days

Document Requirements & Guidelines

File Format Requirements

  • Image Files: JPG, PNG, JPEG (Max size: 2MB)
  • PDF Files: PDF format (Max size: 5MB)
  • Quality: Clear, readable, and complete
  • Language: English or Hindi preferred

Verification Process

  • Documents are verified within 24-48 hours
  • Additional verification may be required
  • You will be notified of any discrepancies
  • Keep original documents for physical verification if needed

Important Notes

  • All documents must be valid and not expired
  • Photocopies must be self-attested
  • Documents in regional languages must have English translation
  • Incomplete applications will be rejected
